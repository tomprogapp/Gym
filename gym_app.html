Quick rules
Train each session at least 48 hours apart — e.g. Mon + Thu, Tue + Fri
Always complete the Knee Prehab section before any lifting
Rest 2–3 min between compound lifts · 90 sec between isolation exercises
Stop 1–3 reps short of failure most sets — push to failure occasionally to calibrate
Add 2.5 kg when you hit the top of the rep range across all sets with good form
Pain is a stop sign — not a push-through signal
6-Day Cycle Logic
Complete sessions 2× per week. Each session = the next uncompleted day in the cycle.
Day 1 → Day 2 → … → Day 6. After Day 6, the full cycle resets and you start from Day 1 again.
Every day has 4 types: 🔵 Knee prehab · 🔴 Strength · 🟢 Tennis (gym) · 🟡 Stretching
To add a tutorial video to any exercise: click the exercise card, then "+ Add Tutorial Video"
